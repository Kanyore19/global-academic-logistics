Why Global Academic Logistics

Tutors who've done the job, not just passed the class

We started Global Academic Logistics because too much online tutoring is generalists reading ahead in the textbook. That doesn't hold up once you're staring at a real assignment on a real deadline.

So we built a smaller, more deliberate roster: working and former professionals in software, nursing, data, and engineering who tutor because they're good at explaining the thing they actually practice. Every match starts with your syllabus, not a generic curriculum.

The result is tutoring that holds up under exam pressure — because the person teaching you has been on the other side of the problem.

Questions

Before you reach out

How fast can I start?

Most students hear back within one business day. After a short intro call to confirm your course and goals, you're typically matched with a tutor within 48 hours.

Will my tutor actually know my specific course, not just the general subject?

Yes — we ask for your syllabus or course outline during matching and pair you with a tutor experienced in that exact curriculum and level, not a generalist working from the textbook.

What if the tutor isn't the right fit for me?

You get a free rematch within the first week, no extra cost and no awkward conversation required — just tell your advisor.

Do you only offer ongoing packages, or can I book a single session?

Both. Book one session ahead of a specific exam, or set up a recurring plan tied to your term — whichever matches how you actually work.

Is this the same as someone doing my assignments for me?

No. Tutors teach the concept and walk through practice problems with you so you can do the work yourself — that's what actually moves your grade and holds up on exams.

What timezones do you cover?

Sessions run online across UK, US, Canadian, Australian, Spanish, and Indian time zones — we schedule around your availability, not ours.
